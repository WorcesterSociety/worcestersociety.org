About

The Worcester Society for the Advancement of Computing Sciences was founded in late 2016 by a group of academically-driven computer science and applied mathematics students. Its primary purpose is to serve towards providing the next generation with the proper tools for prioritizing computer science in today's data-driven world. It aligns with our motto, "Scientia est pabulum," or in other words, "Knowledge is the food."
